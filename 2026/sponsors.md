## Sponsorship Opportunities
If your organization would like to sponsor Clinical NLP, please contact <clinical-nlp-workshop-organizers@googlegroups.com> and let us know your organization's interest.
Depending on the level of support, sponsorship benefits may include:
* Awarding best papers for the main workshop and/or shared tasks.
* Recognition of support of keynote addresses and/or student registrations during the opening session.
* Company logo on printed materials, signage, and website.
