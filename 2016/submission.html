Paper Submission

All submissions must be in PDF format and should follow COLING style guidelines. Submissions may have a maximum length of eight (8) pages for long papers and four (4) pages for short papers, with unlimited pages for references. Both long and short papers will undergo rigorous review. All submissions should be anonymized, and should not include authors' names or any other identifying information. Please submit using the following link: https://www.softconf.com/coling2016/ClinicalNLP.
